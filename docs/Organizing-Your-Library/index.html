🌱 Organizing Your Library

As your library grows, you'll want to tidy it up — group related topics, rename things, and move them around. All of this happens right inside the column browser. This page walks through each action.

💡 Folders nest as deep as you like, and topics (your decks of cards) live inside the deepest folder. There's no limit — organize it however makes sense to you.

Make a new folder

  1. Open the folder you want the new folder to live inside (or stay at Everything for a top-level folder).
  2. At the bottom of the column, click ➕ New folder.
  3. Type a name (e.g. Microbiology) and press Enter.

Example: inside Biology, make a folder called Genetics to hold all your genetics topics.

Rename a folder or topic

  1. Hover over the row (on touch devices the buttons are always visible) and tap the ⋯ More menu.
  2. Choose Rename.
  3. Type the new name and press Enter.

Move something (Cut → Paste)

Use this to relocate a folder or topic to a different place.

  1. Open the ⋯ More menu on the item → choose Cut.
  2. Navigate to the folder where you want it.
  3. Click Paste here at the bottom of that column.

The item moves — it leaves its old home and lands in the new one.

Example: you made Photosynthesis in the wrong place. Cut it, open Cell Biology, and Paste here.

Copy something (Copy → Paste)

Use this to duplicate a folder or topic.

  1. Open the ⋯ More menu → choose Copy.
  2. Navigate to the destination folder.
  3. Click Paste here.

The original stays put and a duplicate (named with a "(copy)" suffix) appears in the new location. Copying a folder copies everything inside it too.

↔️ You can also drag and drop a folder or topic straight onto another folder to move it.

Sort a column

Each column has a Sort by control so you can order its contents however you like:

Sort option Orders by
Name (A–Z) Alphabetical, ascending (the default)
Name (Z–A) Alphabetical, descending
Most cards Fullest topics/folders first
Manual The order you added them in

Sorting only changes how things are displayed — it never moves or changes your actual cards.

Delete something

  1. Open the ⋯ More menu → choose Delete.
  2. Confirm.

♻️ Deleting is safe. Folders, topics, and cards go to the Trash first, so you can restore them if you change your mind. Nothing is gone for good until you empty the trash.

Add cards to a topic

At the bottom of a column you'll also find ➕ Add AI Cards, which jumps straight into the card-creation flow for that location. See Adding Cards.
