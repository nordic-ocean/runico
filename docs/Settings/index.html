🎨 Settings

Choose a canvas theme and the language for your cards and interface.


Looping demo · plays automatically

Open Settings (the ⚙️ gear icon in the top bar) to make Runico yours. There are three things to set: the look, the language, and your AI key.

Appearance — pick a canvas to learn against

The "canvas" is the background color you study on. Pick whatever's easiest on your eyes:

Theme Canvas Best for
Light White Bright rooms, daytime study — calm and clean.
Warm Off-white All-day reading — softer, easier on the eye.
Dark Near-black Low light and night-time study.

The change is instant — tap a theme and the whole app switches.

Language — for the interface and your cards

Runico's interface is available in 7 languages:

  • English
  • Português (Brasil) — Portuguese (Brazil)
  • Português (Portugal) — Portuguese (Portugal)
  • Español — Spanish
  • Русский — Russian
  • Italiano — Italian
  • 中文 — Chinese

📝 Important: this sets the language of the interface (buttons, labels). Your cards stay in whatever language you wrote them in. Switching to Spanish won't translate your existing flashcards — it just changes the app's own text.

AI card generation — your OpenRouter key

This is where you set up automatic card drafting. Under AI card generation you can:

  • Paste and Save your OpenRouter API key (and Test key to confirm it works).
  • Clear the key whenever you like.
  • Choose a Generation model, with a rough "≈ N cards per $1" estimate to help you weigh quality vs. cost.

🔒 Your key is private: stored only on your device in the browser, or encrypted in your OS keychain in the desktop app. Full setup, model choice, and costs are on Using OpenRouter — or skip the key and use your own AI.

A worked example

It's late and your eyes are tired. You open Settings, tap Dark, and the canvas turns near-black instantly. Much better. You tap Done and get back to studying. 🌙
