# Welcome to the Runico Wiki **Magical runes for your learning.**

Runico turns anything you're learning into flashcards and helps you practice them. There's no install and no account — it runs right in your browser. You recall the answer, reveal it to check yourself, then tap Continue or Pause. That's the whole loop. No confusing "how well did you remember?" grading scales.

Brand new here? Start with Getting Started — it gets you from zero to your first study session in a couple of minutes.

▶ Open Runico in your browser — it's free and ready right now

What can Runico do?

Each feature has its own page with a short animated demo, step-by-step instructions, and a worked example. Pick whatever you're curious about:

Feature In one sentence
📖 Studying Cards Recall, reveal, then Continue or Pause — no grading scales.
⏯️ Resuming Your Last Session One tap drops you back on the exact card you stopped on.
🗂️ Browsing and Practicing Navigate subjects → folders → topics, then practice any of them.
🌱 Organizing Your Library Make folders, rename, move, copy, and sort your topics.
🔀 Merging Libraries Fold another .runico file into this one — content and history, with duplicate handling.
📈 Tracking Your Progress See whether a topic is trending up, session by session.
✨ Adding Cards Drop in material and let AI draft cards — or paste your own.
🤖 Using OpenRouter Connect a key so AI drafts cards for you, billed per use.
💬 Using Your Own AI Draft cards for free with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini — no key.
🖼️ Image-Occlusion Cards Hide labels on a diagram to study figures, anatomy, and maps.
🔎 Seeing the Source Open the original material, with the studied term highlighted.
🎨 Settings Choose a canvas theme and the language for your cards and app.

The 30-second version

  1. Open Runico and create a new library (a file that holds all your cards).
  2. Add some cards — drop in a page of notes, a screenshot, or a textbook figure.
  3. Study — recall each answer, reveal it, then Continue or Pause.
  4. Come back any time — Runico remembers exactly where you stopped.

That's it. Welcome aboard. ✨
