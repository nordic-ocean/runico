# 3 · Browse & practice

> Navigate subjects, folders, and topics in a column browser, then practice any topic.

<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/browse.gif" alt="Browse & practice — looping demo" width="700">
  <br><sub>Looping demo · plays automatically</sub>
</p>

Your whole library lives in a column browser that drills down from broad to specific:
**subjects → folders → topics**. Pick a topic and you can practice the whole thing, or
just the cards that are due.

## Walkthrough

1. **Your whole library lives in a column browser.** Subjects → folders → topics.
2. **Open a subject.**
3. **Drill into a folder.**
4. **Pick a topic to see its details.**
5. **Practice the whole topic — or just what's due.**

## What you see

As you drill in, new columns slide into view:

- **Today** — _19 cards due today_ → Biology (19 due), Languages (6 due), History
- **Biology** — _19 due · 184 cards_ → Cell Biology (11 due), Genetics (7 due), Ecology (1 due)
- **Cell Biology** — _11 due · 64 cards_ → Organelles · Ch. 4, Membrane transport, Mitosis · five phases, Photosynthesis
- Each column has a **Practice all** action for studying everything in it.

Selecting a topic opens an **action card** with its stats (_22 cards · 4 due · last 9d ago_),
a resume hint (_Paused at card 2 · 3 of 4 remaining_), and actions:
**Continue practice**, **Open cards**, and **Add more AI Cards**.
