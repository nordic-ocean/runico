# 3 · Browse & practice

> Navigate subjects, folders, and topics in a column browser, then practice any topic.

<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/browse.gif" alt="Browse & practice — looping demo" width="700">
  <br><sub>Looping demo · plays automatically</sub>
</p>

Your whole library lives in a column browser that drills down from broad to specific:
**subjects → folders → topics**. Pick a topic and you can practice the whole thing, or
just the cards that are due.

## Walkthrough

1. **Your whole library lives in a column browser.** Subjects → folders → topics.
2. **Open a subject.**
3. **Drill into a folder.**
4. **Pick a topic to open its card.**
5. **The card leads with its accuracy trend.** A glance at how its recent sessions are going — in place of the old spark icon.

## What you see

As you drill in, new columns slide into view:

- **Today** — _11 cards due today_ → Biology (11 due), Chemistry, History · Modern Era
- **Biology** — _11 due · 52 cards_ → Cell Biology (11 due), Genetics, Ecology
- **Cell Biology** — _11 due · 52 cards_ → Organelles · Ch. 4, Membrane transport, Mitosis · five phases, Photosynthesis
- Each column has a **Practice all** action for studying everything in it.

Selecting a topic opens an **action card**. It leads with a small **accuracy-trend**
sparkline — a glance at how the topic's recent sessions are going — then its stats
(_22 cards · 4 due · last 9d ago_), a resume hint (_Paused at card 2 · 3 of 4 remaining_),
and actions: **Continue practice**, **Open cards**, **View progress**, and
**Add more AI Cards**. (See [Track your progress](04-track-your-progress.md) for the full
trend view.)
