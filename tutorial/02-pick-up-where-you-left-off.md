# 2 · Pick up where you left off

> The home view remembers your last session and shows how many cards remain.

Runico greets you on the home view with the practice you were last in, so getting back
into study is a single tap — no hunting for where you stopped.

## Walkthrough

1. **Runico remembers the practice you were in.** It greets you on the home view.
2. **Paused mid-deck? It shows how many cards remain.**
3. **One tap drops you back on the exact card.**
4. **Finished a deck? The same action becomes _Restart_.**

## What you see

A single **quick-resume** action on the home view that adapts to your last session:

| State | Action | Detail |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Paused mid-deck | **Continue practice** | _Photosynthesis · 3 cards remaining_ |
| Deck finished | **Restart practice** | _Photosynthesis_ |
| Not yet started | **Begin practice** | _Membrane transport_ |

Tapping it drops you straight onto the exact card you left off on.
