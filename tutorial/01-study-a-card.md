# 1 · Study a card

> Recall the answer, reveal it, then Continue or Pause — no grading scales to fuss over.

Studying in Runico is deliberately simple. A card shows a prompt with the key idea
blanked out. You recall it from memory, reveal the answer to check yourself, and then
make one of two choices. There's no grading scale to fuss over.

## Walkthrough

1. **A card shows a prompt with the key idea blanked out.** Recall it before revealing.
2. **Tap _Show answer_ to check yourself.**
3. **The answer is revealed — now two simple choices.**
4. **_Pause_ stops the session and saves your place.**
5. **_Continue_ advances to the next card.**

## What you see

- A cloze prompt, e.g. _"The **[nucleus]** contains the cell's genetic material."_ — the
  blanked term is hidden until you reveal it.
- A progress marker like **1 of 4**.
- Before revealing: a **Show answer** button (plus a quiet **Pause**).
- After revealing: two buttons — **Pause** and **Continue**.
- A footer with a **Source** link (_Cell Biology · Ch. 4_) and keyboard hints:
  **Enter** to continue, **Esc** to pause.
