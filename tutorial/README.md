# A quick tour of Runico

Eight short, looping demos — one per feature. Everything plays automatically.

<img src="assets/study.gif" alt="A Runico study session — looping demo" width="700" />

</div>

---

Runico turns anything you're learning into flashcards and helps you practice them —
recall the answer, reveal it, then Continue or Pause, with no grading scales to fuss over.
This tour walks through the eight features that make up the experience — from studying a
single card to making the app your own.

Each page below mirrors one of the looping demos from the in-app feature tour:
a short description, then a step-by-step walkthrough of exactly what happens on screen.

## The tour

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| 1 | [Study a card](01-study-a-card.md) | Recall the answer, reveal it, then Continue or Pause — no grading scales to fuss over. |
| 2 | [Pick up where you left off](02-pick-up-where-you-left-off.md) | The home view remembers your last session and shows how many cards remain. |
| 3 | [Browse & practice](03-browse-and-practice.md) | Navigate subjects, folders, and topics in a column browser, then practice any topic. |
| 4 | [Track your progress](04-track-your-progress.md) | See whether a topic is trending up — per-session accuracy over time, with a drill-down to any single card. |
| 5 | [Add cards from anything](05-add-cards-from-anything.md) | Drop in material, let Runico draft cards, then keep the ones worth studying. |
| 6 | [Mask an image](06-mask-an-image.md) | Draw, drag, and resize boxes over a figure to build image-occlusion cards. |
| 7 | [See the source](07-see-the-source.md) | Open the original material as a book page, with the studied term highlighted in context. |
| 8 | [Make it yours](08-make-it-yours.md) | Choose a canvas theme and the language for your cards and interface. |

## At a glance

- **Study without grading scales.** Reveal the answer, then choose **Continue** or **Pause** — that's it.
- **Always resumable.** Runico remembers the exact card you stopped on.
- **A library you can navigate.** Subjects → folders → topics, in a column browser.
- **See your progress.** Per-session accuracy charts show whether a topic is trending up — reporting only, never grading.
- **Cards from any material.** Drop in a page, screenshot, or notes and Runico drafts cards for you to review.
- **Image-occlusion cards.** Mask regions of a figure to study diagrams and labels.
- **Source in context.** Every card links back to the original material, shown like a book page.
- **Make it yours.** Light / Warm / Dark canvas themes and per-language cards and interface.
