# Welcome to the Runico Wiki

**Magical runes for your learning.**

</div>

Runico turns anything you're learning into **flashcards** and helps you practice them.
There's no install and no account — it runs right in your browser. You recall the
answer, reveal it to check yourself, then tap **Continue** or **Pause**. That's the whole
loop. No confusing "how well did you remember?" grading scales.

> **Brand new here?** Start with **[Getting Started](Getting-Started)** — it gets you from
> zero to your first study session in a couple of minutes.

### ▶ [Open Runico in your browser](https://nordic-ocean.github.io/runico/app/) — it's free and ready right now

## What can Runico do?

Each feature has its own page with a short animated demo, step-by-step instructions, and
a worked example. Pick whatever you're curious about:

| | Feature | In one sentence |
|---|---------|------------------|
| 📖 | **[Studying Cards](Studying-Cards)** | Recall, reveal, then Continue or Pause — no grading scales. |
| ⏯️ | **[Resuming Your Last Session](Resuming-Your-Last-Session)** | One tap drops you back on the exact card you stopped on. |

## The 30-second version

1. **[Open Runico](https://nordic-ocean.github.io/runico/app/)** and create a new library (a file that holds all your cards).
2. **[Add some cards](Adding-Cards)** — drop in a page of notes, a screenshot, or a textbook figure.
3. **[Study](Studying-Cards)** — recall each answer, reveal it, then Continue or Pause.
4. **Come back any time** — Runico remembers exactly where you stopped.

That's it. Welcome aboard. ✨
